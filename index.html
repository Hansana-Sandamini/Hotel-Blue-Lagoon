About Us

Welcome to Blue Lagoon

Where luxury meets the ocean

Nestled along a pristine private beach on the crystal-clear waters of the Mediterranean, Hotel Blue Lagoon offers an unforgettable escape with impeccable service, exquisite dining, and breathtaking panoramic ocean views. Our award-winning resort blends contemporary elegance with tropical charm to create the perfect vacation experience for discerning travelers.

Since opening in 2010, we've become renowned for our personalized service and attention to detail. Our 120 luxurious rooms and suites feature private balconies, many with direct ocean views, and are appointed with the finest linens and amenities. The resort spans 12 acres of lush tropical gardens with three swimming pools, including our signature infinity pool that appears to merge with the sea.

Our culinary team, led by Michelin-starred chef Antonio Rossi, creates unforgettable dining experiences across our five restaurants and bars. From fresh seafood at our beachfront grill to innovative Mediterranean fusion cuisine at our rooftop restaurant, every meal is a celebration of local flavors and ingredients.

Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway, family vacation, or corporate retreat, our dedicated staff will ensure your stay is nothing short of extraordinary. Our concierge team can arrange everything from private yacht charters to guided hiking tours of the nearby coastal trails.

Blue Lagoon is more than just a hotel - it's a sanctuary where memories are made and the stresses of everyday life melt away with each ocean breeze. We invite you to experience the magic for yourself and discover why our guests return year after year.
